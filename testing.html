Benefits of AR in Medical Education

    
1 Enhanced Visualization: AR provides interactive 3D models of anatomy, helping students understand complex structures and spatial relationships.

2 Surgical Simulation: Students can practice procedures on virtual models, improving skills safely before real-life application.

2 Better Engagement & Retention: Interactive AR experiences boost learning engagement and improve memory retention compared to textbooks.

3 Real-Time Feedback: AR delivers instant feedback during simulations, helping learners correct mistakes and build confidence.

4 Global Accessibility: AR enables remote medical training, making quality education available to students and professionals worldwide.

5 Ongoing Professional Training: Healthcare providers can refresh skills and learn new techniques without leaving their workplace.
